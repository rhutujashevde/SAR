Sophisticated Attendence Record

Sophisticated Attendance Record (SAR) is an application (app) which will be used to store and retrieve students attendance. This would eliminate almost all the manual work required to collect and maintain attendance records. SAR would let teachers record attendance directly to the database through their mobile phone. This data could be retrieved by any user of the SAR app.
